Design System Overview

An user-friendly, open source and beautiful design system made for speed of workflow as well as providing consistency overall the design of the product.

Design System Credit

This design system was created for the research propose by the author and coded by himself for the experiemntal and usage propose as opensource

Design Principles

Along with design system some principles which have been applied for the design are necessary enough for the evaluation of the design decisions where some of the design principles that was established and will be along with the process of our creating design are as below.

Distraction Free

As visual identity over every element are design can cause the distraction for the whole system. Taking into consideration of the distraction free design it needed to be implemented it over the colors, shadows, line buttons and other design elements of the system.

User Centric

Within the layover of the center and core of design interface where all of the necessary guidance of the user belonging and onboarding can be carried out.

Platform Value Oriented

The value over software developments elements has been accomplished and the required shareable design has been created for cross browsing functionality.

Familiar Tone of Voice

As the system may require the design enhancement over future purpose the team onboarding for new member as well as existing member should be familiar with the tone of voice and terminology over the development such as button has terminology of (btn), Dialog as (Modal), Alerts as 44 (Alerts) and many more
